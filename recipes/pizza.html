Pizza

What is Pizza?

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough.

It is usually topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Make the base
  2. Make the Sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Bake! and enjoy
